====================================
  Web App Creator - Quick Guide
====================================

1. Double-click run-webapp.bat
2. Enter the Website URL (example: https://github.com)
3. Enter the App Name (example: GitHubApp)
4. Wait until build is finished.
5. Find your new app inside the folder created.

Note:
- Requires internet connection on first run.
- Windows 10/11 recommended.
